About us

The holding includes two wine companies "Suvorov - Vin" (2003) and "Kazayak - Vin" (1958). The first association with the "Suvorov - Vin" holding is a wide range of wines and modern technologies. We own 500 hectares of vineyards, located in the southern part of Moldova, where the climate is perfect for quality grape growing. The companies strategy is to reveal to the whole world the exceptional quality of Moldovan wines, through skillful marketing and various cultural aspects of Moldova

The holding produces a wide range of dry, semi-dry, semi-sweet, dessert wines, as well as natural fruit wines, mulled wine (natural infusion of 16 medicinal herbs and red currants), punch (natural wine with added fruit juices), wines with low alcohol content, sparkling wines , vermouths and liqueurs. Our portfolio is revealed in impressive collections such as: Green Lee, Storks, Kazayak, Nicole, Bon Voyage, Sang de Terre, Monteco, etc.

2008 for „Suvorov – Vin”

Marked the rise of the company to a new level, becoming a leader in the production and export of bottled wines from the Republic of Moldova, with an annual volume of about 10 million bottles and 20% of the total export volume. Our wines have found vocation in countries such as Poland, Germany, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Lithuania, Latvia, Romania, Croatia, Bosnia, Belarus, Ukraine, Russia, Kazakhstan, Israel, Greece, Ghana, Nigeria, China, Japan, USA, and of course same in Moldova

Category „IGP"

Our wines are produced in strict accordance with European winemaking requirements. Since 2015, red wines have been granted the IGP category (Protected Geographical Indications). The company applies modern methods and technologies for processing grapes using membrane filtration, cold processing and sterile bottling processes. The production process is subject to constant monitoring in combination with the secular traditions of Moldovan winemaking, practical experience and innovative technologies

Chardonnay Barrik

  • Vintage: 2019
  • Wine type: White dry
  • Grapes: 100% Chardonnay

It is a soul project, in which the word "Ambitious" has a strong personal philosophy.
A wine turned melody - a symphony of thoughts, feelings and tradition.
So, a fulfilled and rich Chardonnay style from Kazayak.
Chardonnay possesses a light golden colour, surprises with rich aroma of white flowers, grapefruit and ripe apricot nuances. The persistent and crisp palate offers a play between complexity and freshness with a finish that impresses on any occasion. The wine is full-bodied and creamy, mineral with hints of barrel, with good ageing potential.
The winemaker's mastery and attention to detail bring out the quality of this distinctive Chardonnay.
We recommend drinking it at 8°-9°C to enjoy maximum aromatic intensity.
It pairs ideally with white meats, fish dishes, sophisticated appetizers or aromatic salads, but also forms a harmonious pairing with melted cheese, white bread, fruit, olives, ﬁsh, nuts and sweets.
Manufactured under a food quality and safety management system in accordance with
ISO 9001, ISO 22000

Pinot Noir Barrik

  • Vintage: 2019
  • Wine type: Red dry
  • Grapes: 100% Pinot Noir

It is a soul project, in which the word "Ambitious" has a strong personal philosophy.
A wine turned melody - a symphony of thoughts, feelings and tradition.
So, a style of Pinot Noir from Kazayak - the Holy Grail of the wine world.
Pinot Noir possesses a red color with crimson nuances, seduces with a complex bouquet with nuances of black cherry, blackberry, strawberry, underlined by brownish, earthy and spicy aromas. Elegant palate, lightly velvety, silky, lingering aftertaste with fine truffle nuances.
It is one of the most versatile wines bringing an added nobility and elegance to every meal, but can also be served on its own.
As suggestions it goes excellently with a variety of cheeses, fish dishes with baked legumes, mushroom rizzoto with asparagus, baked quail stuffed with dried fruit and rosemary, as well as with fruit desserts.
We recommend consuming it at a temperature of +18°+20°C to enjoy maximum aromatic intensity.
Produced under a food quality and safety management system in accordance with
ISO 9001, ISO 22000
