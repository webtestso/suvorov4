The Concours Mondial de Bruxelles

The Concours Mondial de Bruxelles is an international competition where thousands of wines are entered by producers to be tasted and judged by a panel of experts. Our seasoned judges from around the world taste entries with a single-minded ambition, and that is to select wines of irreproachable quality with no bias stemming from the label or an appellation’s prestige. The competition is one of the most significant international events of its kind.
